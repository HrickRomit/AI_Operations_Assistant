1. Project Overview
The AI Operations Assistant is a multi-module SaaS platform designed to replace repetitive manual tasks in
small and medium businesses (SMBs). It combines document intelligence, automated customer support,
lead processing, and admin automation — all accessible through a clean web dashboard. The platform is
built on a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) engine and is extensible via n8n workflow automation.
Problem Statement
Small businesses in Bangladesh and globally struggle with repetitive operational tasks: answering the same
customer questions, manually processing leads, onboarding new staff, and generating routine documents.
Hiring staff for these tasks is expensive; most SMBs lack the budget or technical team to deploy AI solutions
themselves. This platform solves that gap.

3. Core Features (MVP)
Module 1
Document Intelligence (RAG Engine)
 Users upload company documents: PDFs, Word docs, policy files, SOPs, manuals.
 
 Files are chunked, embedded, and stored in a FAISS or Chroma vector store.
 
 Users can ask natural-language questions; the AI retrieves relevant chunks and answers with source
 citations.
 Supports multi-document workspaces per business account.
 
 Admin can tag, delete, and update documents through the dashboard.
 
Module 2
Customer Support AI
 Embeds FAQ knowledge into a chat interface that businesses can use internally.
 
 Answers customer queries using only the company's uploaded documents.
 
 Falls back gracefully when the answer is not in the knowledge base.
 
 Logs all unanswered queries so the business can fill knowledge gaps.
 
 Optionally integrates with n8n to push queries from email/WhatsApp into the assistant.
 
Module 3
Email Draft Generator
 User pastes a raw customer email or inquiry into the interface.
 
 AI generates a professional reply based on company tone and uploaded context.
 
 User can edit, regenerate, or copy the draft with one click.
 
 Optional tone selector: Formal, Friendly, Concise.
 
 Can be automated via n8n: incoming Gmail → AI draft → saved as draft in Gmail.
 
Module 4
Lead Summarizer & Qualifier
 User pastes a raw lead inquiry or form submission.
 
 AI extracts: name, interest, budget, urgency, and key questions.
 
 Outputs a structured lead card: summary, recommended action, priority score.
 
 Leads are stored in a leads database within the dashboard.
 
 Optionally automated via n8n: form submission → AI summary → CRM entry.
 
Module 5
Internal Knowledge Base
 Acts as a smart onboarding assistant for new staff.
 
 Staff can ask: 'What is our refund policy?' or 'How do I handle a complaint?'
 
 AI answers from company-uploaded docs with exact source references.
 
 Reduces onboarding time significantly for growing teams.
 
 Admins can version-control documents (update old → archive old chunks).
 
Module 6
Admin Report & Summary Generator
 User pastes meeting notes, raw data, or a transcript.
 
 AI generates a clean structured summary with action items and highlights.
 
 Can produce: weekly summaries, meeting minutes, client reports.
 
 Optionally triggered via n8n on a schedule (daily/weekly digest).

Honest Risk Assessment
■ Over-engineering risk
Start with Module 1 (Doc Q&A;) only. Ship it. Then add modules
one at a time.
■ API cost risk
Set per-user query limits. Use caching for repeated queries.
Monitor OpenAI spend weekly.
■ Competitor risk
You're not competing with ChatGPT. You're serving SMBs who
want a ready-made tool for their business, not a general AI.
■ Scope creep
Use this PRD as your contract with yourself. Do not add features
outside this document until v1 is live.
